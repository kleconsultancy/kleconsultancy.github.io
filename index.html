About Us

KLE Consultancy Pte. Ltd. is a Civil and Structural Engineering Consultant specializing in providing Professional Building Facade Engineering Consultancy Services.

Different from others, our projects are carried out by UK/SPE International Certified and registered Specialist Professional Facade Engineers working together with experienced Local Professionals.

We provide services globally, in association and collaboration with both international and local professional companies, ensuring we maximize our capabilities to serve clients better and better.

  • Construction Management
  • Building Maintenance and Management, Façade Inspection, BCA Periodic façade inspection with Façade A&A,R&R Technical/project management and consultancy services.
