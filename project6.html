Garden Waterfront I & II

  • Garden Waterfront I @ Tengah will come with shops, a supermarket, restaurants, an eating house, and a residents’ network centre. Childcare centres, playgrounds, and fitness stations are found in both Garden Waterfront I @ Tengah and Garden Waterfront II @ Tengah to provide added convenience. Roof gardens are located on some of the residential blocks and on both Multi-Storey Car Parks (MSCPs). In Garden Waterfront II @ Tengah, the roof garden on the MSCP is linked to nearby residential blocks via sky terraces.
  • 18 residential blocks, ranging from 9 to 16 storeys in height. 2,077 units of 2-room Flexi, 3-, 4-, and 5-room flats.
  • Owner: Housing & Development Board.
  • Role: Façade Engineering Consultancy Services for façade contractor for varies type of Facade, Aluminum Works, Glass Window, Cladding, Canopies, and Structural Steelworks.
